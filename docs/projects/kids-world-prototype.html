Big Picture

The tablet is the window. Server3 is the brain.

Kids World runs as a fullscreen child shell on Android. The tablet handles touch, mic, sound, and display. Server3 later handles the LLM, mission logic, safety rules, watch-time balance, and approved content decisions.

Behind The Scenes

Later Server3 flow

1. Voice capture Tablet records the child’s request and ships it to Server3.
2. LLM guidance The model rewrites the request into a safe structured art or story job.
3. Result cards The child gets big follow-up buttons instead of a scrolling chat log.

Design Intent

Rewards stay bounded and visible.

Not an app store There are no feeds, tabs, social loops, or recommendation holes.
Not a raw chatbot Voice and LLM live inside guided rooms like Create or Ask.
Not pure bribery Rewards are one layer. Progress, creation, praise, and visible mastery should matter too.
